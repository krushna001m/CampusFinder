Frequently Asked Questions

How long do you keep found items?

+

We keep found items for 60 days. After that period, unclaimed items are donated to charity or disposed of according to university policy.

How do I prove an item is mine?

+

You'll need to provide a detailed description of the item, including any identifying marks or features. For valuable items, we may ask for proof of ownership such as receipts, photos, or serial numbers.

Can I report a lost item if I'm not on campus?

+

Yes, you can report lost items through our website or by calling our office. If your item is found, we can arrange for it to be shipped to you (shipping fees may apply).

What happens if I find someone's ID or credit card?

+

IDs and credit cards should be turned in immediately to the Lost and Found office or campus security. We have special procedures for handling these sensitive items and contacting their owners.
